About Huskko

Turning Waste into Useful Furniture

Every year, millions of tons of rice husk are thrown away or burned, causing air pollution and waste. Huskko Chair changes that. We use rice husk — a byproduct of rice milling — and combine it with recycled plastic to create a strong, lightweight, and eco-friendly chair.

By choosing Huskko, you help reduce agricultural waste and support a greener future. It is a simple product with a big impact — good for homes, schools, and the environment.

What It's Made Of

Natural & Recycled Materials

🌾

Rice Husk

The outer shell of rice grains. Usually thrown away — we turn it into the main material of our chair.

♻️

Recycled Plastic

Used plastic is collected and reprocessed to form the structure of the chair — reducing plastic waste.

🧪

Small Amount of Resin

A small amount of resin is added to bind the materials together and make the chair stronger and longer-lasting.
